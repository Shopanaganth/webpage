Sri Lankan A/L Engineering Technology Stream


Subjects in the Engineering Technology stream

The Engineering Technology stream in Sri Lanka is an academic pathway offered at the Advanced Level (A/L) in secondary education, tailored for students with a keen interest in engineering and technological fields. The core subjects in this stream include:

  • Engineering Technology
  • Science for Technology

Students are required to study at least two of the core subjects mentioned above. The third subject can be selected from the following options:

  • Accounting
  • Mathematics
  • Home Economics
  • English
  • Geography
  • Communication and Media Studies
  • Information & Communication Technology
  • Art
  • Business Studies
  • Agricultural Science

The Engineering Technology stream is designed for students aiming to pursue careers in engineering, technology, and related fields. It provides a strong foundation in technical knowledge and practical skills that are essential for understanding and working within the rapidly evolving technological landscape.

This stream emphasizes problem-solving, innovation, and the application of scientific and technological principles. By studying subjects such as Engineering Technology and Science for Technology, students gain hands-on experience and theoretical knowledge that prepare them for further education and careers in engineering, technology, manufacturing, and design.

The Engineering Technology stream is an excellent choice for students who are passionate about technology and engineering, offering numerous opportunities for further education and professional development in these dynamic fields.

The Engineering Technology (ET) stream in Sri Lanka focuses on applying engineering principles and technologies to solve practical problems. This stream prepares students for careers in various fields of engineering technology, including mechanical, civil, and electrical engineering. The following universities offer programs in Engineering Technology:

1.Engineering Technology (ET)

  • University of Sri Jayewardenepura
  • University of Kelaniya
  • University of Jaffna
  • University of Ruhuna
  • Rajarata University of Sri Lanka
  • Uva Wellassa University of Sri Lanka
  • Wayamba University of Sri Lanka
  • University of Colombo
  • Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka

2.Information Communication Technology (ICT)

The Information Communication Technology (ICT) stream emphasizes the use of technology in managing and processing information. This stream covers various aspects of computer science, information systems, and communication technologies. Students can pursue careers in IT, software development, and digital communications. The following universities offer programs in Information Communication Technology:

  • University of Sri Jayewardenepura
  • University of Kelaniya
  • University of Ruhuna
  • Rajarata University of Sri Lanka
  • South Eastern University of Sri Lanka
  • University of Vavuniya, Sri Lanka
  • University of Colombo
  • Uva Wellassa University of Sri Lanka

Admission and Academic Focus

Admission to the programs listed above is typically based on academic merit, with students selected according to their examination results and academic performance. Both Engineering Technology and Information Communication Technology streams offer students comprehensive training in their respective fields, preparing them for successful careers in technology and engineering industries.
