Sri Lankan NVQ Level Courses


NVQ Providing Institutions in Sri Lanka

In Sri Lanka, the National Vocational Qualification (NVQ) system is supported by a range of institutions that offer vocational training and education. These institutions are responsible for providing NVQ programs in various fields, helping students gain practical skills and recognized qualifications.

1. Tertiary and Vocational Education Commission (TVEC)

Oversees and accredits NVQ programs and institutions.
Website: TVEC Sri Lanka

2. Vocational Training Authority (VTA)

Provides vocational training programs and NVQ certifications.
Website: VTA Sri Lanka

3. Sri Lanka Institute of Advanced Technological Education (SLIATE)

Offers NVQ programs in various technological fields.
Website: SLIATE

4. Department of Technical Education and Training (DTET)

Provides technical education and NVQ programs through various technical colleges.
Website: DTET Sri Lanka

5. National Apprentice and Industrial Training Authority (NAITA)

Focuses on apprenticeships and industrial training programs leading to NVQ certifications.
Website: NAITA

6. Skills Development Fund (SDF)

Supports skills development projects and NVQ training programs.
Website: SDF

7. Polytechnic Institutes

Offer a range of NVQ courses in technical and vocational fields.
Examples include Colombo Polytechnic Institute and Kandy Polytechnic Institute.

8. National Institute of Business Management (NIBM)

Provides NVQ programs in business management and related fields.
Website: NIBM

9. Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs)

Offer NVQ programs in various industrial and technical disciplines.

10. Various Private Training Institutes

Accredited private institutions also offer NVQ programs in specialized fields.


NVQ Level 1 & 2

Basic skills courses to provide foundational knowledge and skills required for various fields.

  • Basic Welding
  • Basic Electrical Work
  • Basic Carpentry
  • Basic Plumbing
  • Basic Masonry
  • Basic Automobile Repair
  • Basic Tailoring

NVQ Level 3 & 4

Courses for semi-skilled and skilled worker levels, offering more in-depth training.

  • Advanced Welding
  • Electrical Installation
  • Carpentry and Joinery
  • Plumbing
  • Masonry
  • Automobile Mechanic
  • Textile and Garment Technology
  • ICT Technician
  • Graphic Designing
  • Food Processing Technology
  • Hotel Operations
  • Beauty Culture

NVQ Level 5 & 6

Courses for diploma level, focusing on supervisory and advanced technical skills.

  • Mechanical Engineering Technology
  • Electrical and Electronic Engineering Technology
  • Civil Engineering Technology
  • Quantity Surveying
  • Construction Technology
  • Software Development
  • Network Technology
  • Mechatronics Technology
  • Automobile Engineering Technology
  • Hospitality Management
  • Business Management
  • Agriculture Technology

NVQ Level 7

Courses for bachelor degree level, providing advanced and specialized knowledge in various fields.

  • Bachelor of Technology (BTech) in Engineering
  • BTech in Information Technology
  • BTech in Construction Technology
  • BTech in Agriculture Technology
  • BTech in Food Technology
  • BTech in Hospitality Management
